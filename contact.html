Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our AI marketing services

AI marketing uses machine learning algorithms to analyze vast amounts of data in real-time, enabling predictive insights, hyper-personalization, and autonomous optimization that traditional methods can't match. While traditional marketing relies on human analysis and manual adjustments, our AI systems continuously learn and improve from every data point, identifying patterns and opportunities invisible to human analysts.

Most clients see measurable improvements within 30-45 days as our AI models complete their initial learning phase. However, performance continues to improve over time as the system gathers more data. Paid media campaigns typically show the fastest results, while SEO and content strategies may take 3-6 months to reach full potential (though often outperform traditional approaches within the first 60 days).

We integrate with your existing marketing platforms (Google Ads, Meta, CRM, analytics, etc.) and can work with any first-party data you have. The more quality data we can access, the better our models perform, but we can start with minimal data and build over time. We also enrich your data with third-party signals and our proprietary datasets to fill any gaps.

We require a 6-month minimum engagement to allow sufficient time for our AI models to learn and optimize. Most clients see such significant value that they continue working with us long-term - our average client relationship lasts 3.2 years. We don't believe in locking clients into multi-year contracts though - after the initial 6 months, you can cancel with 30 days notice.

We establish clear KPIs during onboarding based on your business objectives - whether that's revenue, leads, ROAS, CAC, or other metrics. Our dashboard provides real-time visibility into performance against these goals. We also conduct quarterly business reviews to assess overall impact and adjust strategies as needed.
